We're thrilled to announce that **Keepin' App is now live** and ready for you to use!

After months of development and testing, we're proud to launch Keepin' App - a comprehensive solution for tracking your health, mind, and wealth all in one place.

## What is Keepin' App?

Keepin' App is a free personal tracking application that helps you monitor:
- **Health**: Exercise and weight tracking
- **Mind**: Journaling and mental wellness
- **Wealth**: Finances, net worth, and budgeting

## Why We Built It

We noticed that people were using multiple apps and spreadsheets to track different aspects of their lives. Keepin' App brings everything together in one intuitive, privacy-focused platform.

## Get Started Today

Start tracking your life today - completely free! Visit [app.keepintracks.com](https://app.keepintracks.com) to create your account and begin your journey.

We're excited to see how Keepin' App helps you achieve your goals!
