Take control of your financial future with Keepin' App's comprehensive financial tracking features.

## Track Income and Expenses

Easily log your income and expenses to get a clear picture of your cash flow. Categorize transactions and see where your money goes.

## Monitor Net Worth

Track your overall financial health by monitoring your net worth over time. See how your assets and liabilities change as you work toward your financial goals.

## Budget Management

Set budgets for different categories and track your spending against them. Get alerts when you're approaching your limits to help you stay on track.

## Financial Insights

Get valuable insights into your spending habits:
- Monthly spending trends
- Category breakdowns
- Savings progress
- Financial goal tracking

## Visualize Your Progress

Beautiful charts and graphs help you visualize your financial journey. See your progress at a glance and identify areas for improvement.

## Secure and Private

All your financial data is encrypted and stored securely. Your information is never shared with third parties.
